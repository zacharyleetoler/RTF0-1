Wearing the government costume while committing the violation. The badge, the robe, the title identifies the violation — it does not protect it.
Denied Imagination
Coined term
What is denied inside gets projected outside. The label is not a description of you — it is an accurate description of what lives in the projector.
Duplicitas
Latin: duplicity, two-faced
Civil rules to make the order, criminal force to enforce it. Both hands take. Neither hand protects.
Your experience: Family court — civil hearing, criminal jail for non-compliance.
Fait Accompli
French/Legal: already done
The act was accomplished before the process that was supposed to authorize it. Duration is not authorization.
Your experience: The arrest before the warrant. The custody change treated as established before any court ordered it.
Implied Opposites
Framework term
Whatever they repeat is what they cannot survive. Whatever they announce loudly is what they are doing quietly. Every label is a confession.
Mens Rea
Latin: guilty mind
Here inverted — the language of their documents reveals their intent. The guilty mind is in the document. Read it there.
Minutatim
Latin: little by little
Extraction by small degrees. Not punishment — resource management. Each fold takes something specific. The accumulation is the mechanism.
Sine Nomine
Latin: without name
The individual author disappears behind the institutional noun. "The Court finds" — no human identified, no accountability possible.
Sua Sponte
Latin: on its own motion
Nobody asked. No lawful predicate existed. The actor decided on their own initiative and built the paperwork around the decision.
Tumey Disclosure
Legal term — Tumey v. Ohio, 273 U.S. 510 (1927)
A decision-maker with a financial interest in the outcome must disclose that interest before the proceeding begins. Failure to disclose voids the proceeding.
Void
Legal term
Never had legal force. Not flawed — never born. Cannot be appealed because appeal accepts the premise that the order was valid.
Wummons
Coined term
A hybrid instrument using summons language (notification, appearance, rights) while functioning as a warrant (arrest, detention, force). The clerk converted a notification request into arrest authority without documented authorization. It performs due process while eliminating it.
